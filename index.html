FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your leads qualified?

Homeowners provide their address and specific service needs. We verify they're in your service area before sending the lead.

How quickly do I get leads?

Leads are delivered within 1 hour of submission. You'll get an email with all details.

What if a lead doesn't convert?

We verify contact info, but if a homeowner doesn't respond after 3 attempts, we'll issue a credit for that lead.

Can I pause or cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts. Cancel anytime. For weekly/monthly plans, you can pause or cancel before the next billing cycle.
